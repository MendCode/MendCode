# Code of Conduct

MendCode is a technical community. Be direct, but keep it useful and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good intent until there is evidence otherwise.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, docs, product behavior, or user outcome.
- Credit upstream work and community contributions.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing reproduction steps and context.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Posting private information or credentials.
- Repeated low-effort noise after maintainers ask for clarification.
- Public exploit details for private vulnerabilities.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly harm the project or community.
